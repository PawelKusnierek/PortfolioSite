Short bio

Hi! My name is Pawel, I am a self-taught web developer with professional experience in both front and back-end development.
I have lived in the UK from the age of 12 and completed my Masters degree in Mathematics at the University of Birmingham in 2016 and have worked in web & software development since.
I started by working in banking, designing and implementing stress test risk models in VBA. Shortly after, I moved to work on my first professional web application where I worked as a full-stack developer.
I have worked on multiple projects since and picked up a varied skillset that makes me well equipped to tackle a wide variety of problems and projects.
I truly enjoy every aspect of web development and feel lucky to be able to do what I love for a living.
